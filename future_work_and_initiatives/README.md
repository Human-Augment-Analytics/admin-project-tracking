## Current Initiatives

1. **Structured Blocker Escalation Protocol**  
   Focuses on improving early detection and resolution of blocked tasks within the *R for Evolution* team by establishing a lightweight, structured escalation workflow.

2. **Weekly Reporting Workflow Clarification**  
   Consolidates the weekly reporting process into a single procedural reference, improving clarity, transparency, and adherence without introducing new rules or tools.

## Adding New Initiatives

New initiatives may be proposed by:

* CS-8803 students collaborating with HAAG  
* HAAG team members identifying workflow improvement opportunities  

Each proposed initiative should include:

* Purpose and motivation  
* Operational context  
* Observations or discovery notes  
* Workflow or procedural description  
* Pilot design and measurement criteria  
* Evaluation criteria and intended impact  

---

## Usage

* Browse the folder to view each initiative document.  
* Follow procedural guidance for ongoing initiatives where applicable.  
* Reference pilot design and measurement sections to evaluate effectiveness of new initiatives.
