# Initiative Operations Reflection

## **1. Reoccurring Tasks Within the Project Group**

- Reviewing weekly reports and extracting **completed, in-progress, and blocked tasks**  
- Maintaining and updating the **operations log**  
- Tracking progress, blockers, and dependencies across researchers  
- Creating weekly summaries (notes, progress, next steps)  
- Building dashboards to visualize task distribution and blockers  
- Coordinating communication directly between researchers and faculty  

---

## **2. Assessing Your Role as Project Manager**

- Providing structure and continuity in the absence of a dedicated CLEAR advisor  
- Acting as a bridge between technical work (researchers) and strategic direction (faculty)  
- Improving visibility into progress, risks, and dependencies through structured reporting  
- Enabling earlier identification of risks (e.g., timeline feasibility, validation gaps)  

---

## **3. Challenges & Successes Observed in the Group**

### **Challenges**
- Lack of a CLEAR advisor role has required more direct coordination with faculty  
- Ongoing restructuring causing temporary misalignment (adding researchers late in the project is not always optimal)  
- Initial timeline expectations not fully aligned with current pace  
- Researchers often use the term **“blocker”** broadly to label any task taking longer than expected, rather than true impediments  

### **Successes**
- Strong technical progress and implementation of core analysis functions  
- Improved codebase structure, testing, and reproducibility practices
- Effective collaboration despite time zone differences, staggered onboarding, and a distributed team structure

---

## **4. Collaboration With Stakeholders**

- Communicating directly with faculty on Discord in the absence of a CLEAR advisor  
- Translating technical updates into structured summaries for decision-making  
- Proactively raising risks and facilitating discussions on scope and priorities 
- Supporting researchers by clarifying expectations and reducing ambiguity  

---

## **5. Feedback Received on Your Role**

- Positive feedback on clarity and organization of weekly logs and summaries  
- Recognition that reporting has improved visibility into team progress 
- Informal alignment from faculty indicating trust in coordination and communication 

---

## **6. Plans for Enhancing Your Role**

- Introduce more structured milestone planning toward project completion  
- Facilitate estimation and timeline alignment across researchers  
- Enhance dashboards to better surface risks and dependencies  
- Strengthen coordination between researchers and faculty for faster decision-making  

---

## **7. Support Needed**

- Clarification of responsibilities typically handled by a CLEAR advisor  
- Establish clear **task ownership** to replace self-assignment and increase visibility of ongoing work before weekly reports  
- Clearer **time estimates** for tasks from researchers to support planning  
- Clear expectations for communication timelines and structure between PM and researchers  
- Standardized weekly report format with commitment to timely submission by researchers
