# Milestones and Larger Themes

## Overview

This folder contains documentation that helps organize HAAG project progress at two levels:

* **Milestones**: concrete, time-bounded targets used to track execution and delivery
* **Larger Themes**: broader strategic directions that connect related milestones across projects and semesters

Together, these help teams align short-term work with long-term research goals.

---

## What to Document

### Milestones

Milestone documentation should clearly describe:

* Objective and expected outcome
* Scope and ownership
* Timeline and target dates
* Dependencies and risks
* Completion criteria

### Larger Themes

Theme documentation should capture:

* Strategic focus area and motivation
* Related projects or teams
* Key research or operational priorities
* Expected impact
* Indicators of progress over time

---

## Suggested Usage

* Use milestones to monitor near-term execution and accountability.
* Use larger themes to maintain continuity across iterations and semesters.
* Reference this folder during weekly reporting, advisor check-ins, and roadmap planning.
* Update entries as scope, priorities, or constraints evolve.

---

## Adding or Updating Entries

When adding a new document, include:

* Title and context
* Owner(s) and collaborators
* Current status
* Relevant dates
* Links to supporting artifacts (issues, reports, dashboards, or notes)

Keep entries concise, operational, and easy to review asynchronously.

---

## Intended Audience

* **Primary:** Project Managers and student researchers
* **Secondary:** Computational Advisors and mentors
* **Tertiary:** HAAG Leadership and collaborators
